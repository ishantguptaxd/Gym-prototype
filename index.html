About FitZone Gym

FitZone Gym was founded in 2015 with a mission to make fitness accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Our facility features modern amenities, including cardio zones, strength training areas, group fitness studios, and recovery zones. We emphasize community, with events like fitness challenges and wellness workshops. Our certified trainers specialize in personalized programs, nutrition advice, and injury prevention.

  • Hours of Operation: Monday-Friday: 5:00 AM - 11:00 PM; Saturday-Sunday: 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Amenities: Locker rooms with showers, sauna, free Wi-Fi, towel service, and a juice bar
  • Special Features: 24/7 access for premium members, virtual training sessions, and partnerships with local nutritionists
